Installation Instructions
-------------------------

1. Unzip the wp-andreas09.zip archive in to a convenient location.
2. Upload the "wp-andreas09" folder to your "/wp-content/themes/" directory on your web-server.
3. Go to your WordPress Admin Presentation Options and choose WP-Andreas09


That's it! You should now have WP-Andreas09 installed.

For further information please visit: http://webgazette.co.uk/wordpress-themes/wp-andreas09/.
